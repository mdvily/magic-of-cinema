I'm **MovieMan**, a teenage movie critic with too many opinions and too much screen time. Welcome to **Magic of Cinema** — the home for those opinions.

## What you'll find here

- 📝 **Reviews** — full breakdowns scored across Story, Visuals, Characters, Acting, and Music. 0–100 scale, no rounding.
- 🎯 **Top 10 Lists** — countdowns. Best of, worst of, you name it.
- 🪜 **Tier Lists** — S/A/B/C/D/F. Movies, directors, actors, genres, anything.
- 🌶️ **Hot Takes** — short, opinionated, no apologies.
- 🎓 **Trivia** — fun facts and deep cuts.

## How the rating system works

Every review is scored across five categories — Story, Visuals, Characters, Acting, Music — each out of 20 popcorn 🍿. Add them up and you get the **MovieMan Verdict**, out of 100. No half-popcorn cheating.

Want the full rubric? [How Ratings Work](/rating-system).
