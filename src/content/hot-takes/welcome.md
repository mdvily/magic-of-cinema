Hi. I'm **MovieMan**, and this is my new movie blog.

I'm gonna review movies. Old ones, new ones, good ones, bad ones. I'm gonna make Top 10 lists, drop hot takes, post fun trivia, and build tier lists for every actor, director, and genre I can think of.

Each review gets scored across five categories — Story, Visuals, Characters, Acting, Music — each out of 20 popcorn buckets. Add them up and you get my final verdict out of 100. No rounding. No half-popcorn.

Subscribe by checking back. Comment if you have something to say. Don't be a Neck No. 💀
