# Story

# The story is simple, however, that somewhat is part of its charm. Yes, it has aged somewhat, however the comedy remains humorous. In fact, this might bu the funniest film of the 90’s. I also love the heart. It must succeed, due to its themes of friendship, and sure enough, it does. VERDICT: 18/20

# Visuals

# This is not an animated movie, however, the live-action element certainly is colorful, perfectly affecting the tone. The Good Burger cinematography also ages quite well. However, my favorite part is the clothes the characters wear, due to them fitting the characters perfectly. VERDICT: 20/20 

# Characters

# Our protagonist Dexter Reed is a high school slacker, assigned to work at the Good Burger fast food place, due to him having accidentally crashing the car of his teacher, Mr. Wheat. I first went into the film, thinking that he would be just some dumb trope of “wreckless teen” but they did him right, by giving him an arc, of the meaning of competence and friendship. Ed, our idiotic cashier becomes smarter, and is just a delight. Otis works the deep fryers, and could have more of an arc, but still works as hilarious. Mr. Baily is the boss of Dexter and Ed, who learns to be less hard on them, for they can do right, despite their occasional stupidity. Monique, Dexter’s love interest is a talented employee, showing girl power is a great thing, in my opinion. Fight me. The villain, Kurt, is a greedy, typical, despite hilarious antagonist, with a motive that makes sense. Mr. Wheat is an energetic teacher, who is crucial to the story. VERDICT: 18/20

# Acting

# Kenan Thompson as Dexter Reed is humorous, and dumb. Same as Kel Mitchell as Ed, however his kindness dualogue shines. Abe Vigoda as Otis is kind, and humorous. Dan Schneider as Mr. Baily is strict, yet fair, and has quite the range. Shar Jackson as Monique has a fluid identity. Sinbad as Mr. Wheat is a bit too full of life, but still works due to his appropriate amount of dedication. Jan Schweiterman as Kurt Bozwell is sinister, and humorous. Every actor feels like they know their characters, and want to be here. VERDICT: 19/20

# Music

# The main theme has hip-hop and funk to it, and it affects the tone pretty well. The music sounds similar to what it is supposed to sound like, understanding its tone well. The sinister scenes are dark, and the fun scenes are… well… fun. VERDICT: 20/20

# Sometimes, a dumb movie can be a good one. If you like Dumb And Dumber, then you should check this out. It may eventually age, but it has not, yet. Welcome to, magic of cinema, home to magic of cinema, can I take your verdict? Final Score: 95%
