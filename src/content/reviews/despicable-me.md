# Despicable Me. Illumination’s first movie, and considered one of its finest. Yes, that bar is very low, however even the strictest of critics consider this a pretty good movie. But how good is it? SPOILERS!

# Story

# The story of the movie is simple. Gru wants to steal the moon, so he adopts girls to accidentally help him. However, Gru learns that being a villain gets him nowhere. The story is simple, and I do occasionally feel like it may be repetitive and like it rushes to the next joke. However, the comedy still is humorous, and iconic due to its one-liners. However, it is the heart. I cried as Gru stole the moon, and realizes that he wants to be with the girls instead. VERDICT: 14/20

# Animation

# I think that the character designs and animations have somewhat stood the test of time. Yes, the long noses creep me out at times, but I find the animations fluid. The clothings also look very fitting to the characters. But my favorite part of the animation is the special effects. They really did stand the test of time. VERDICT: 18/20

# Characters

# We begin with Gru. As a villain, he is just hilarious. But, he has one of the best character arcs ever. Margo, Edith and Agnes are adorable, and unique in adoption terms, because they get loved, unlike others. Vector is hilarious, although his antics could be improved by a tiny bit. Dr. Nefario is hilarious, and appropriate support for Gru. Mr. Perkins is Gru’s cause for evil, and a force to be reckoned with. My only real gripe is that the minions have nothing to do with the story, so I find them overrated. VERDICT: 19/20

# Acting

# Every single actor here cares so much about their roles, and are fully dedicated to be here. Steve Carrell as Gru is hilarious, and this might be one of the best portrayals in all of animation. He is sarcastic, yet loving. He also has amazing chemistry with Miranda Cosgrove, Dana Gaier, and Elsie Fisher as Margo, Edith and Agnes are adorable and can be sarcastic. Jason Sigel as Vector is perfectly full of energy. Russel Brand as Dr. Nefario is fluid in scientific words. Will Arnett as Mr. Perkins is menacing, and a force to be reckoned with. And Pierre Coffin as the minions. What can be said. VERDICT: 20/20

# Music:

# The main theme of the movie is iconic. It fits both the funny and serious scenes perfectly, via sounding different. But when it’s the scene where Gru chases Vector’s ship sounds intense. My only real gripe is that it kind of relies too much on Pharrell Williams music. It fits the scene well, however the film could rely on its main theme more. VERDICT: 19/20

# The film is just amazing. Yes, the story needs improvement, but it still works, with the characters, acting, animation and music are amazing. Final Score: 90%
