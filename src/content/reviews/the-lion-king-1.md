**The Lion King 1 ½: Hakuna Matata**. It is a movie about Timon And Pumbaa watching the movie of how they came to be. This film is actually one of the most acclaimed Disney Mainstream Sequels. However, that is a very low bar, due to the latter not mostly being very good.

Music

Well, the score certainly exists. I wish it did not. The single most memorable thing about it is that it for one scene rips off The Good, The Bad, And The Ugly, which was 38 years prior to the film. Nothing else. The most of the music does nothing to be memorable. “That's All I Need” and “Grazing in the Grass“ feel like they are just here to check out the boxes, without being anything special.
