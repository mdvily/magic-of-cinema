# **The Lion King 1 ½: Hakuna Matata**. It is a movie about Timon And Pumbaa watching the movie of how they came to be. This film is actually one of the most acclaimed Disney Mainstream Sequels. However, that is a very low bar, due to the latter not mostly being very good. But is this a good film? SPOILERS!

Story
Why does everyone think that humorous means good? The movie is funny, as many of the jokes are standing the test of time, as of right now, however, the rest of it is not the best. Its comedy is so dedicated to being there, that the heart between the characters is overlooked. The story does nothing to be different, as Timon and Pumbaa raise Simba and they save the Pride Lands, with the meerkat and warthog never even having been there before. However, WHY WAS THE WHOLE MOVIE JUST TIMON AND PUMBAA WATCHING A MOVIE?! VERDICT: 6/20
====================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================================

# Animation

# The Animation of the franchise has really fallen off, has not it? The color looks delightful, but I hate the character animations. They make the characters move like walking monsters out of the mud. The action scenes do the bare minimum every time. I also think that while the character designs look good, they were lacking. VERDICT: 9/20

# Characters

# Timon and Pumbaa are the protagonists. They do nothing really with their characters, as they just raise Simba, with no character struggles whatsoever. Simba and Nala are crucial to the story, yet, they feel like just plot devices. Ma and Uncle Max are funny, though and actually have solid character arcs, of letting their son/nephew be himself. The hyenas though, are easily my favorite part of the movie, as they are threatening and a challenge to our heroes. VERDICT: 10/20

# Acting

# Nathan Lane and Ernie Sabella as Timon the Meerkat and Pumbaa the Warthog are humorous enough, but they have not the best chemistry. Matthew Broderick and Moria Kelly as the protagonistic lions are basically the same deal. Robert Guillaume as Rafiki in is humorous, along with Julie Kavner and Jerry Stiller as Ma and Uncle Max. However, I hate the portrayals of Whoopi Goldmerg, Cheech Martin and Jim Cunnings as the antagonistic hyenas. They keep trying to be funny, when they should have been scary, due to the narrative of the movie. VERDICT: 11/20

# Music

# Well, the score certainly exists. I wish it did not. The single most memorable thing about it is that it for one scene rips off The Good, The Bad, And The Ugly, which was 38 years prior to the film. Nothing else. The most of the music does nothing to be memorable. “That's All I Need” and “Grazing in the Grass“ feel like they are just here to check out the boxes, without being anything special. "Hakuna Matata" and "The Lion Sleeps Tonight" feel like they are just here to make callbacks. They work a bit in the film’s favor, but I feel like the film is too stupid to be anything else. At least "Grazing in the Grass" and "Digga Tunnah" / "Digga Tunnah Dance" are fun bops. VERDICT: 8/20

# To conclude, I feel like the Lion King trilogy is just getting worst. Yes, it has not reached awful status yet, but I find this overrated. The excuse, “It’s Good, Because It’s Funny” for me does not work here, due to me actually disliking this movie. Final Score: 44%
