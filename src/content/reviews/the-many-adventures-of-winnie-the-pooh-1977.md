**The Many Adventures Of Winnie The Pooh (1977)**. As a compilation of three previously released shorts: Winnie the Pooh and the Honey Tree (1966), Winnie the Pooh and the Blustery Day (1968), and Winnie the Pooh and Tigger Too (1974), this movie has a 1977 release, being considered as the final film Walt Disney had any involvement in, prior to his death in 1966. Now, as a young boy from 2014-2018, I was an avid fan of the Winnie The Pooh books. I personally have a lot of nostalgia in them. However, what about the movie 37 years before I started reading the books? Well, the reviews are quite positive, but I do find this movie overrated. Now, I know, that sounds wrong on paper, but this is just my opinion and why I dislike it. If you like this movie, I am happy for you, but I do not, and I will tell you why. SPOILERS!
