The **Wilhelm Scream** is a stock sound effect — a guy yelling — that's been used in over 400 movies and TV shows since 1951.

It first appeared in *Distant Drums* (1951), where a soldier gets eaten by an alligator. Sound designer Ben Burtt rediscovered it in the '70s and started slipping it into Star Wars. Since then it's been in:

- Every Star Wars movie (yes, every one)
- The Lord of the Rings trilogy
- Indiana Jones
- Pirates of the Caribbean
- Toy Story
- Reservoir Dogs
- Inglourious Basterds
- *and 400+ more*

It's basically a secret handshake between sound designers. Once you've heard it, you'll catch it everywhere.
